Features

Fast Barcode Scanning

Camera-based barcode scanning using CameraX and ML Kit for near-instant recognition.

OpenFoodFacts Lookup

Automatically fetch product data & ingredients from OpenFoodFacts database.

Allergen & Side-effect Detection

Alerts for common allergens (milk, nuts, soy, gluten) and mapped side-effect warnings.

Nutrition Traffic Lights

Color-coded sugar/fat/salt indicators to help make healthier choices at a glance.

How it works

1

Open app & tap scan

Grant camera access, point at the barcode, and let the scanner read it.

2

Fetch product data

App checks OpenFoodFacts for product name, ingredients and nutriments.

3

See results instantly

Traffic lights, ingredient list, and allergen warnings are displayed.
